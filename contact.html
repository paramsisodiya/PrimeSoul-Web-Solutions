Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

A standard 5–8 page website typically takes 7–14 days. Complex projects like e-commerce stores or school portals can take 3–6 weeks depending on features and your response time for content.

Do you use templates or build from scratch?

We build everything from scratch — clean HTML, CSS and JavaScript. No WordPress templates, no page builders. This means faster loading, better SEO, and full control over every detail.

What do you need from me to get started?

We'll need your business details, logo (if available), content/text for pages, reference websites you like, and a brief on your goals. Don't worry — we guide you through everything step by step.

Do you provide support after the website is launched?

Yes! Every project includes a support period (1–6 months depending on plan). We also offer ongoing monthly maintenance packages for updates, security, and content changes.

Can you help with domain and hosting setup?

Absolutely. We guide you through choosing and purchasing a domain and hosting, and we handle the full deployment. You don't need any technical knowledge — we take care of it all.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, NEFT/IMPS, and PhonePe/GPay. Payment is typically split — 50% upfront to start and 50% on final delivery. This keeps it fair and low-risk for both parties.

Still have questions?

Just send us a WhatsApp message — we typically respond within an hour during business hours.
